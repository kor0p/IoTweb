About band

Pikkardiyska Tertsiya is a unique collective for Ukrainian musical culture. During his rapid singing career, this male band has rightfully won the title of "music legend of Ukraine" and its members have become recognized as "meters of acapella singing". Since 1992, the year of its foundation, "Tertius" has released fourteen genre albums and collections. Composition of the formation: Vladimir Yakimets, Yaroslav Nudyk, Bogdan Bogach, Andriy Kapral, Andriy Shaval and Roman Turyanin. The Pikkardiyska Tertsiya repertoire contains more than 300 songs in a variety of musical styles: pop, rock, classic, folk, jazz, country, spiritual music, rock and roll, blues… 95 percent of this is the Ukrainian-language repertoire. Although in general there are songs in 12 (!) Languages ​​in the song act of the sextet. Equally impressive is the geography of the formation's formations: Poland, Germany, Spain, Italy, France, Canada, the United States, Belgium, Switzerland and even Singapore. In 2008, the formation was awarded the National Prize of Ukraine. T. Shevchenko.
